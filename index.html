My name is Alireza Karduni.

I am a researcher in Human Computer Interaction, Visualization, and Computational Social Science.

Currently, I am a Postdoctoral Scholar at Northwestern University's Departments of Computer Science and Psychology. I study how humans interact with information on social media from both social/urban and individual perspectives. I design tools to help humans make better decisions in a variety of uncertain situations.
